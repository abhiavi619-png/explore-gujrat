Somnath Temple

About Somnath Temple

Somnath Temple is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites in Gujarat. Known for its magnificent architecture and spiritual significance, it attracts thousands of visitors each year.

Situated along the Arabian Sea coast, the temple has a rich history and is considered a Jyotirlinga shrine.
